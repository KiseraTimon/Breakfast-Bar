About this dish

Our Chicken Biryani is prepared using the traditional "Dum" method, where the meat and rice are slow-cooked together in a sealed pot to preserve the flavors. We use premium long-grain Basmati rice and fresh, locally sourced chicken. The spice blend involves saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a rich aroma that fills the room.

Each serving is approximately 450g, sufficient for one person with a healthy appetite. It comes in eco-friendly packaging that keeps the food hot for up to 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Premium Basmati Rice
  • Fresh Chicken Thighs & Breast
  • Saffron infused milk
  • Whole Spices (Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon)
  • Fried Onions
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander
  • Yogurt marinade
  • Ghee
